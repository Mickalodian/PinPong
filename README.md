# 🏓 PingPong Game

Welcome to **PingPong Game** – a fun multiplayer and single-player table tennis experience where you can challenge friends and unlock awesome rewards!

## 🎮 Features

- 🤖 **Play Against a Bot**
  - Practice your skills against an AI opponent.
  - Great for improving before challenging friends.

- 👥 **Play With Friends**
  - Invite your friends using a unique **Friend Invite Code**.
  - Join matches quickly and compete in real time.

- ⭐ **Earn Points**
  - Win matches to collect points.
  - The better you play, the more rewards you can unlock.

- 🛍️ **Reward Shop**
  - Spend your earned points on exciting in-game rewards.
  - Unlock cosmetics and other fun items.

- 🎉 **Have Fun**
  - Enjoy fast-paced PingPong matches with simple controls.
  - Perfect for casual games or competitive play.

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Launch the game.
2. Choose to:
   - Play against the Bot, or
   - Play with a Friend.
3. If playing with a friend:
   - Share your Friend Invite Code.
   - Enter your friend's code to join their game.
4. Win matches to earn points.
5. Visit the Reward Shop to spend your points.
6. Keep playing and have fun!
